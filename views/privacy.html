Privacy Policy

We respect the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how Emma collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information Collection and Use

  • Personal Information: Emma collects the user's username, profile picture, and email for the purpose of user differentiation and display in the dashboard.

2. Data Storage

  • Chat History: Emma does not store any chat history. We respect the privacy of our users and do not retain any personal chat data in our database.

3. Data Security

  • Encryption: We utilize MongoDB for storage, ensuring data is encrypted to the highest standards, minimizing the risk of data breaches.

4. Age Restrictions

  • Age Requirement: While there are no strict age restrictions, we recommend users be above the age of 10 to properly engage with Emma as a friendly chatbot.
